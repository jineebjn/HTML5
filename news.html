When working with dynamic content that includes a footer, a problem sometimes occurs where the content on a page is not enough to fill it. The footer, rather than staying at the bottom of the page where we would want it to stay, rises up and leaves a blank space beneath it. For a quick fix, you can absolutely position the footer at the bottom of the page. But this comes with its own downside.
